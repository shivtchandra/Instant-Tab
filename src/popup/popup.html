Instant Screenshot

Capture instantly or use delayed mode for dropdowns.

Current View = visible part only. Full Page = auto-scroll whole page.

Dropdown flow: click Delay 3s, reopen dropdown, capture happens automatically.

Area flow: click Capture Area, drag on page, release to preview.

Long Capture flow: Start, scroll manually, reopen extension, then Finish.

Use preview actions to copy image or download to Screenshots folders.

Shortcuts: Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+S (visible), Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+F (full page)
